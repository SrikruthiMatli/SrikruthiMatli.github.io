Hi! I'm Srikruthi Matli.

I'm currently a graduate student pursuing a Masters of Science in Computer Science with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction at the Georgia Institute of Technology.
Before coming to Georgia Tech I was working in Fidelity Investments as a Front-End Engineer. Previously, I did my undergrad in Electronics and Communications Engineering at VNIT, Nagpur. I will be working as a researcher at the Social Dynamics and Wellbeing Lab at Georgia Tech starting Spring 2022.

I enjoy researching, designing and developing digital products that can satisfy user requirements. I believe interacting with users to understand their needs and incorporating their feedback throughout the design process can help create better interfaces.

I'm an avid journaler and scrapbooker. I love to read fiction in my free time. Fun fact, I volunteer as an editor for asian novels being translated to english.
